## Usage Guidelines

### You MAY

- Use "HMG" in plain text to refer to the HMG product or protocol
- State that your product "implements the HMG Protocol" or "is compatible with HMG"
- Use the HMG name in documentation, blog posts, or educational materials to
  describe or review the product
- Link to official HMG websites and repositories

### You MAY NOT

- Use HMG trademarks in your product name, domain name, or logo without written
  permission
- Imply official endorsement, certification, or affiliation without authorization
- Modify HMG trademarks or logos
- Use HMG trademarks to mislead users about the origin of a product

### "HMG Compatible"

Third-party implementations may claim **"HMG Compatible"** only if they pass the
HMG conformance test suite (`hmg-certification`).

### "HMG Certified"

Only implementations that pass the official HMG certification process may use
the **"HMG Certified"** mark. Certification requires:

1. Passing all conformance tests
2. Compliance with the HMG protocol specification
3. Governance and audit trail verification
4. Review and approval by HMG AI
